School experience details

We are very excited about welcoming those interested in training to teach to Holland Park School. On offer are: some lesson visits; informal opportunities to meet with teachers in the staff room; meetings with school leaders; training sessions (on safeguarding, behaviour
management, lesson planning); opportunities to teach. We supervise candidates throughout the experience, so that DBS checks are not required.

- As a twice outstanding school and with a track record of exceptional GCSE results, we thrive on training new members of staff. We are always looking to make connections with driven and talented graduates who are keen to commit to what is both a highly demanding and highly fulfilling profession.
