School profile

Holland Park School

The Learning Alliance is a cross phase multi academy trust in Cheshire East. Knutsford Academy is our Lead School for ITT, in partnership with LJMU. We are offering school experience days across all five of our secondary schools during 2021 - 2022. This includes the following schools: Knutsford Academy, Congleton High School, The Oaks Academy (Crewe), Sir William Stanier (Crewe) and the Crewe UTC.

About our school experience

Details

We are very excited about welcoming those interested in training to teach to Holland Park School. On offer are: some lesson visits; informal opportunities to meet with teachers in the staff room; meetings with school leaders; training sessions (on safeguarding, behaviour management, lesson planning); opportunities to teach. We supervise candidates throughout the experience, so that DBS checks are not required.

Start and finish times

9am to 3:30pm

Dress code

Business dress, Cover up tattoos, Remove piercings, Smart casual

Teacher training

We train teachers through the Royal Borough Teaching School Alliance.
